Overview

Research internship (University of Amsterdam)

Dec 15, 2022

During my internship at the University of Amsterdam, I contributed to a validation study of online webcam-based eye tracking (WebGazer.js), replicating classic paradigms such as the cascade effect, novelty preference, and the visual world paradigm.

Master thesis: pupil size and response inhibition

Dec 15, 2022

My master thesis examined whether (and to what extent) pupil size predicts successful response inhibition, motivated by an inverted U-shaped relationship between locus coeruleus activity and inhibition performance.
