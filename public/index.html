How do we perceive and interact with ultrasound mid-air haptics?

Hi! My name is Quinn Cabooter, and I am a doctoral researcher at imec-mict-UGent.

I hold a MSc. in Theoretical and Experimental Psychology (Ghent University), and I am currently studying the human perception and interaction with ultrasound mid-air haptics. My research focuses on objective evaluation methods for mid-air haptics, combining behavioral experiments with physiological measures. This website provides an overview of my current research, publications, and ongoing work.
